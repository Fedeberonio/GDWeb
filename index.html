🌱 ¿Por qué? 🌱

🌱 Why? 🌱

Green Dolio nace para acercar productos frescos, naturales y de calidad directamente del productor a tu mesa, cuidando el planeta y apoyando a la comunidad local. Creemos en una alimentación saludable, sostenible y personalizada, sin intermediarios ni desperdicios. ¡Por eso hacemos lo que hacemos!

Green Dolio was created to bring fresh, natural, high-quality products directly from the producer to your table, caring for the planet and supporting the local community. We believe in healthy, sustainable, and personalized eating, with no middlemen or waste. That's why we do what we do!

How it works?

Sobre Nosotros

About Us

En Green Dolio, nos apasiona conectar a las personas con alimentos frescos y saludables directamente de la huerta. Nuestro compromiso es ofrecer productos de la más alta calidad, cultivados con amor y respeto por la naturaleza.

At Green Dolio, we are passionate about connecting people with fresh and healthy food directly from the garden. Our commitment is to offer the highest quality products, grown with love and respect for nature.

Nuestra misión es hacer que comer sano sea fácil, accesible y delicioso para todos.

Our mission is to make healthy eating easy, accessible, and delicious for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions
🛒 Orders & Products
How do I place an order?

You can place your order through our online form or by writing to us directly on WhatsApp. You can also follow us on Instagram @green_dolio to see our available products.

What types of boxes do you offer?
  • Caribbean Fresh Pack: $650 (3 days)
  • Island Weeksential: $990 (1 week)
  • All Green-clusive Mix: $1990 (2 weeks)
Can I customize my box?

Absolutely! Each box is assembled according to your tastes and what is freshest on the day of your order. Just tell us what you like and what you don't.

Do you offer "à la carte" orders?

Yes, contact us via WhatsApp with your specific order and we will send you availability and a quote.

What extra products are available?

We offer free-range eggs, quinoa, baba ganoush, honey, olive oil, chimichurri, hummus, natural juices, and more. Check our full catalog or ask us on WhatsApp.

🚛 Deliveries
Where do you deliver?
  • Juan Dolio: Minimum order $650
  • Santo Domingo: Minimum order $990
  • Boca Chica: Minimum order $990
What days do you deliver?

We deliver every day from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays are FREE. Other days have a delivery charge of $100.

How far in advance should I order?

Preferably 24 hours in advance, or until 12:00 PM on the same day of delivery.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes, we offer free delivery in our coverage areas when you meet the minimum order.
